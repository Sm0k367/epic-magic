# FLOORBURNER Remotion Visualizer

**Location**: `/workspace/media-swarm/remotion/`

This is a **deterministic**, perfectly synced music visualizer built with Remotion (React). It follows the exact 8-minute structure of your track and eliminates all the problems of pure AI video generators:
- No random spoken audio or lip movements
- Perfect beat sync
- Frame-accurate timing to your drops, builds, and breakdown
- High-energy club atmosphere with fire, lasers, dancing crowd silhouettes, and massive "FLOORBURNER" title impacts

## How to use

1. `cd /workspace/media-swarm/remotion`
2. `npm install`
3. `npm run dev` (for preview)
4. `npm run render` (to export MP4)

The video is 1920x1080, 8 minutes long, 40fps.

**Key Features**:
- Bass-reactive fire and scaling effects on drops
- Pulsing lasers and lighting synced to 130 BPM
- Section-aware intensity (massive on Drop 1 + Drop 2)
- Clean, realistic club aesthetic (no cartoon junk)

The code is fully commented and ready. You can easily customize colors, add your actual audio track, or extend it with real video footage.

This is the professional way to generate a video that actually matches your story.
